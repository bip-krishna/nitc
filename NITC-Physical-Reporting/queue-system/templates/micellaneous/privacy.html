Privacy Policy

Admission Reporting & Token Booking Portal

This Privacy Policy explains how the Admission Portal collects, uses, and protects student information. By using this portal, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal details (name, application ID, email, contact number)
  • Academic records and certificates
  • Payment verification details
  • Uploaded documents for admission processing

2. How Information Is Used

  • To verify eligibility and process admissions
  • To schedule reporting and slot bookings
  • To communicate important admission updates
  • For internal record-keeping and audits

3. Data Protection

We implement reasonable security measures to protect student data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no digital system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

4. Data Sharing

Student information is not sold or shared with third parties except:

  • When required by law or regulation
  • For official academic or government verification
  • With authorized institutional departments

5. Data Retention

Information is stored only as long as necessary for admission and academic purposes, after which it may be securely archived or deleted.

6. Student Responsibility

Students must ensure submitted information is accurate. The portal is not responsible for issues caused by incorrect data provided by users.

7. Cookies & Technical Logs

The portal may use basic cookies or server logs to improve performance, security, and user experience. These do not track personal browsing behavior.

8. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated when necessary. Continued use of the portal means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact

For privacy-related concerns, students may contact the admissions office or portal administrator.
