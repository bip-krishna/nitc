Guidelines for Physical Reporting

Admission Reporting Instructions for Students

Welcome

Students who have completed online admission formalities must report physically at the campus for document verification and final confirmation of admission. Please follow the guidelines below to ensure a smooth process.

1. Reporting Date & Time

  • Report strictly within your booked time slot.
  • Arriving too early or late may delay your verification.
  • Keep your slot confirmation accessible (print or phone).

2. Documents to Carry

  • Original Class 10 mark sheet & certificate
  • Original Class 12 mark sheet & certificate
  • Category certificate (if applicable)
  • Admission fee payment receipt
  • Government-issued photo ID
  • Passport-size photographs (2â€“4 copies)

3. Dress Code & Conduct

  • Wear decent and appropriate attire.
  • Maintain discipline and follow staff instructions.
  • Parents/guardians may accompany if permitted.

4. Campus Rules

  • Follow campus security guidelines.
  • Do not carry prohibited items.
  • Respect queue systems and reporting order.

5. Health & Safety

  • Follow current health advisories if any.
  • Carry personal medications if required.
  • Inform staff in case of medical needs.

6. Important Notes

  • Failure to report may lead to cancellation.
  • Incomplete documents can delay admission.
  • Final admission is subject to verification.

Need Help?

Contact the Admissions Help Desk for any clarification before your reporting date.
